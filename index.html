Yes, under FDA guidelines, healthcare providers can legally import approved medications from licensed Canadian pharmacies for patient use. We provide all necessary documentation for compliance.

How is cold-chain validated?

We use temperature-controlled packaging with data loggers that record temperature throughout transit. All shipments include temperature reports and are insured for product integrity.

How is pharmacovigilance handled?

Our licensed pharmacists follow Health Canada's adverse event reporting protocols. We maintain detailed records and coordinate with both Canadian and US health authorities as required.
